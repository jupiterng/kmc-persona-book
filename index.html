How to Use This Book

Your voice is
your brand asset.

This persona book is the single source of truth for how each KMC executive shows up on LinkedIn. It translates each leader's authentic identity into a consistent, compelling content framework — built for business owners, decision-makers, and the professionals they want to attract, inspire, and convert.

Every page captures one leader's distinct voice, content pillars, tone guidelines, no-go zones, and signature phrases. Ghost writers and content teams should treat each persona as a living brief — not a script, but a compass.

Voice, not vocabulary

Don't copy the exact words. Internalize the tone, the worldview, and the things that leader genuinely cares about. Then write from that place.

One audience, one message

All content is written for business owners — from SMEs to enterprise — who are evaluating partners, seeking insights, or building their own teams. Speak to their growth challenges.

No two voices alike

Each leader has a distinct brand. Avoid blending voices. Every persona's differentiator is a strategic asset. Protect it.

01

Read the full persona

Study their tone, their story, their red lines before writing a single word.

02

Choose a content pillar

Every post should anchor to one of their defined topics — never generic filler.

03

Apply their format preference

Some prefer storytelling; others favor facts. Match the structure to the persona.

04

Avoid their red lines

Every leader has topics and tones that don't fit their brand. These are non-negotiable.
